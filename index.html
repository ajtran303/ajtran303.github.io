About Me

As a career changer with a background in the restaurant industry, I discovered a passion for coding and spent five years learning as much as I could through self-study and free resources. I even had the opportunity to teach coding to kids in after school programs on a part-time basis.

However, it wasn't until I enrolled in the Turing School of Software and Design's coding bootcamp that I truly fell in love with software engineering. The immersive experience allowed me to learn more in seven months than I had in my previous five years of self-study.

After graduation, I began my career as a Software Development Instructor before transitioning to a role as a Software Engineer. While I enjoy engineering, I also have a deep passion for teaching and hope to return to the classroom someday.

If you have a unique opportunity that aligns with my skills and interests, please don't hesitate to reach out to me at ajtrandev@gmail.com.
